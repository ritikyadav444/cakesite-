We’re a family business who is honored to serve Mumbai. Since 2018, our mission has been simple: to bring smiles to our neighbors. We’re here to help you celebrate life’s moments, big and small: from A+ spelling tests to milestone birthdays.


ABOUT US

A TEAM THAT FEELS A WHOLE LOT LIKE FAMILY


We opened our shop in 2018, and that first day we sold out of every baked good by noon. Over the last three years, we’ve learned a few things (like how to keep up with demand!), but are honored to still delight some of those very same customers.

WE LOVE BEING A BAKERY

To us, a bakery is everything that’s right with a neighborhood. We love working dough in the early hours of the morning, the satisfyingly straight rows of baguette, and too-adorable-to-eat cupcakes. We love putting smiles on the faces of little ones, prepping for the big meeting with a great breakfast, and giving an excuse to lingering with an old friend.

“WE SELL HAPPINESS. IT JUST HAPPENS TO LOOK LIKE A CUPCAKE.”
